🎗️ Give, and your racer feels it

Giving through shoptodogood.com feeds your racer — donations unlock liveries and point multipliers. Your community gets its own division, standings in your newsletter.

Every gift a member makes through ShopToDoGood is credited to their racer. Liveries are cosmetic and permanent once earned; multipliers apply to that racer's points in team totals like the ones above. Nothing here touches the fairness of the race itself — the river doesn't know who gave. It only changes what your duck is wearing, and how much a finish is worth to the team score.

How the fairness holds up

Same as the main game: today's river is generated from the date alone, and every racer's run is computed purely from that river plus their name. Nothing about a team, a donation tier, or who's watching changes the current. Anyone on your roster can be checked independently — type the name, get the same run, every time.

Free to race, every day. No account. No gambling — entry is free everywhere, always, and prizes are points and glory.

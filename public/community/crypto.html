Why this is different from a normal collectible

Most collectibles are a picture with a receipt. This one races — today, tomorrow, and every day after, against whatever the day's river throws at it. The result isn't stored by a company and served back to you. It's computed, fresh, from two public inputs: the date, and the name.

  1. Name locked at mint.Whatever name goes in becomes the racer's identity, permanently. That's the entire "token" — a name and a mint date.
  2. Every day, a new river.The engine deterministically regenerates the day's current from the date alone — same seed, same river, everywhere on Earth.
  3. Anyone can recompute it.Feed the same date and name into the same open engine and you get the same finish, every time. No database to trust, no oracle, no "our servers say so."

Try it — the permanent record

Type any name and pull its last 7 days of finishes, computed live in your browser against the standard 400-racer field. This is the same computation the "collectible" concept above is built on — nothing here is precomputed or fetched from a server.

🏵️ Soulbound trophies

Milestones — a podium finish, a season MVP, a hundred races sailed — mint as soulbound trophies bound permanently to the racer's identity. They can't be sold, traded, or transferred to another name. They exist to say "this racer did this," not to be flipped.

What this is not

  • No wagering. You cannot bet against another racer or the field.
  • No yield. Holding a racer pays no interest, dividends, or staking rewards.
  • No floor price promises. Nothing here is priced, listed, or guaranteed to be worth anything.
  • Identity only. This is a permanent, checkable record of a name's racing history — not a financial instrument.

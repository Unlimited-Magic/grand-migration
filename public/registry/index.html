Free claims vs. Permanent Deeds

Racing a name for free — over at the main track — holds that name only through the current season. When a season ends, every free claim resets and any name can be picked up new.

A Permanent Deed doesn't do that. Once armed, this Racer No. and this name's entire computed career — every heat, past and future — stay assigned to you forever, across every season, whether you ever race again or not.
